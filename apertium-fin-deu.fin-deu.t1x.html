Macros

Macros are helper functions in apertium transfer files.

test

Parametres: 1
  1. let $number ≔ ""

case-mangler

Parametres: 1
    1. if
      1. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <ela>
      2. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <ill>
      3. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <abl>
      4. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <all>
      5. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <par>
      6. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <acc>
      3. then:
      1. let tl[1]['a_case'] ≔ <acc>
    3. elseif
      1. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <ine>
      2. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <ade>
      5. then:
      1. let tl[1]['a_case'] ≔ <dat>
    5. elseif
      1. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <ins>
      2. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <tra>
      3. sl[1]['a_case'] ≟ <ess>
      7. then:
      1. let tl[1]['a_case'] ≔ <nom>

tensemood-mangler

Parametres: 1
    1. if
      1. sl[1]['a_tense'] ≟ <past>
      3. then:
      1. let tl[1]['a_tense'] ≔ <pii>

Rules

The actual rules concerning stuff.

Drop voice from verbs, mangle tense mood

Matching pattern:

  1. verb

Action:

  1. tensemood-mangler( $1 )
  2. Output:
    1. [
      1. tl[1]['lem'] tl[1]['a_verb'] tl[1]['a_tense'] tl[1]['a_prsnum']
      ]vp <VP>

Mangle case of nouns

Matching pattern:

  1. noun

Action:

  1. case-mangler( $1 )
  2. Output:
    1. [
      1. tl[1]['lem'] tl[1]['a_noun'] tl[1]['a_gender'] tl[1]['a_number'] tl[1]['a_case']
      ]np <NP>

Mangle case of adjs

Matching pattern:

  1. adj

Action:

  1. case-mangler( $1 )
  2. Output:
    1. [
      1. tl[1]['lem'] tl[1]['a_adj'] <sint.attr>
      ]np <AP>

Default rule

Matching pattern:

  1. sent

Action:

  1. Output:
    1. [
      1. tl[1]['whole']
      ]sent <SENT>
